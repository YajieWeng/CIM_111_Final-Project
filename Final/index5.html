Founded

After the end of world war ii, football enjoyed a worldwide Renaissance. However, there were only a few international exchanges and club-level competitions. At this time, Frenchman gabriel ano came up with the idea for the European club cup. The founder of the European footballer of the year campaign, who also writes for France's l 'equipe and France football magazines, has a keen sense of the possibility of a new competition bringing together Europe's top clubs. He proposed the creation of a new league by the world's leading clubs. The promoters of the European club cup have been in contact with FIFA and uefa, but the results have been disappointing. But yarno's plan has been warmly welcomed by several clubs, including real Madrid, anderlecht and Vienna rapid, as well as the Hungarian football federation.

Roform

Before the 1991-92 season, the champions league was a purely elimination game. This season it set up a group stage at the last eight. During the 1992-93 season, the champions league was officially renamed the champions league, and matches were played on Tuesdays and wednesdays. But people used to call it the champions league. In the following years, the competition system of the champions league has changed again and again, gradually applying the grouping cycle competition system to the matches before the rematch. The teams participating in the competition are no longer limited to the league champions of various countries and the champions league champion of last season, and the runners-up of the league of some football powerhouses can also participate in the competition. All these changes are to increase the number of games, so that the participating teams can get more benefits.

Sponsor

Like the World Cup, the champions league is sponsored by some of the biggest companies in the world, and is also associated with some of the biggest events in the world, such as the premier league, copa libertadores and serie a. When the uefa champions league was renamed in 1992, it was set to have a maximum of eight sponsors per tournament, advertised on billboards in matches and sponsored pre-match and post-match visits.
